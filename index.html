FAQs

Clear guidance for partners, buyers, and training clients across pig and snail farming operations.

1. What types of pigs and snails are available?

We operate a program that includes healthy breeding sows, grower/finisher pigs, and piglets raised under strict welfare and biosecurity standards. For snails, we specialize in Giant African Land Snails, maintained in humidity-controlled snailery with proven breeding cycles and high-quality stock for fresh and processed products.

2. Farm visits or inspections

Visits by appointment are welcome. Prior notification and safety briefings are provided. Inspectors may review housing, hygiene, welfare standards, feed systems, and animal health records to ensure compliance with our policies.

3. Feeding and breeding process overview

Pig feeding follows a balanced, growth-focused program with automated feeders and clean water. Snail breeding emphasizes humidity control, nutrition, and safe housing with clear genetics and cyclical breeding management to optimize yield and health.

4. Delivery and logistics coverage

We offer nationwide logistics and reliable delivery for bulk orders, with export-ready packaging and cold-chain options where required. Lead times are based on stock and order size; bulk orders receive dedicated planning support.

5. Partnership or bulk buyer inquiries

We welcome partnerships and bulk arrangements. Please contact our partnerships team to discuss volumes, pricing, and terms tailored to your market or distribution network.

6. Training or consultation services

We offer on-site and remote training on pig and snail husbandry, biosecurity, feed management, and farm operations optimization for large-scale producers and distributors.

7. Pricing, payment options, packaging and QA

Pricing is volume-based with flexible payment terms. We provide QA-certified packaging, label compliance, and traceability. Availability is year-round with seasonal adjustments for export readiness; please contact for current pricing and terms.
